#+title: St Raphael the Archangel
#+date: <2023-10-24 10:33>
#+description: 
#+filetags: feasts

O GOD, who gavest blessed Raphael the Archangel unto thy servant Tobias to be the companion of his wayfaring: grant unto us thy servants; that we may ever be protected by the guardianship of this same thine Archangel, and defended by his help. Through the same thy Son Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

The Season is Trinity, the Liturgical Color is Red.
