#+title: The Annunciation of the Blessed Virgin Mary (Transferred)
#+date: <2024-04-08 11:32>
#+description: 
#+filetags: liturgy feasts

O GOD, who didst will that thy Word should take flesh in the womb of the Blessed Virgin Mary at the message of an Angel; grant that we thy supplicants, who believe her to be in truth the Mother of God, may be holpen by her intercessions before thee. Through the same Jesus Christ our Lord, /AMEN/.
