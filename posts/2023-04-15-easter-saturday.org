GRANT, we beseech thee, Almighty God, that we who have devoutly kept the Paschal Feast, may thereby be counted worthy to attain unto eternal joys, Through the same our Lord Jesus Christ. /Amen/.

The Season is Eastertide, the Liturgical Color is White.
