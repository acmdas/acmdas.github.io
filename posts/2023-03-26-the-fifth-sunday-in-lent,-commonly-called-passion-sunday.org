#+title: The Fifth Sunday in Lent, Commonly Called Passion Sunday
#+date: <2023-03-31 15:56>
#+description: 
#+filetags: liturgy Lent

We beseech thee, Almighty God, mercifully to look upon thy people; that by thy great goodness they may be governed and preserved evermore, both in body and soul; through Jesus Christ our Lord. /Amen/.

Passiontide, the two weeks ending on Holy Saturday commemorating the Passion of Christ, begins today.

The Season is Lent, the Liturgical Color is Purple.
